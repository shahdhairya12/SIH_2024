Introduction

Welcome to the Real Time Monitoring of Water Bodies in Delhi. This project is dedicated to providing an online platform for tracking and analyzing the status of water bodies in the city. It allows users to participate in surveys, access monitoring dashboards, and learn about ongoing efforts to rejuvenate these critical resources.
